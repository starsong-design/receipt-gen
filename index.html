markdown: **bold**, *italic*, ~~strikethrough~~. Escape any sigil with \ (e.g. \*, \.).

per-line directives (start of line, with the dot — chain multiple, e.g. .small.center foo):

  • .center / .right / .left — alignment
  • .title — bigger, centered heading
  • .small — smaller text
  • .bold / .strike — whole-line emphasis
  • .rule — dotted rule
  • .row LEFT\tRIGHT — two columns (split on a literal \t)

Lines without a leading .directive render verbatim with leading spaces preserved — type spaces to indent.
